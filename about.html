WHO I AM

Digital Creator

Ehte

Welcome to my website! I'm a multilingual digital creator who is passionate about game development and AI. My name is Ehtesham Ul Haq, and I was born on April 24th, 2005. At 6'2", I stand tall both physically and professionally.

I have a diverse language background and can speak English, Urdu, Punjabi, Finnish, German, Arabic, and Hindi. Being multilingual has allowed me to connect with people from different cultures and has given me a unique perspective on the world.

I started my career as a web developer, but I eventually found my true passion in game development. Currently, I'm working as a game dev on Unreal Engine 5, which has been a dream come true for me. I love being able to create immersive worlds and experiences for gamers.

In addition to game development, I also have experience as a community manager on Discord servers. I've been doing this since 2018 and have learned how to effectively engage with people online and build strong communities.

Moving forward, I'm excited to continue my career in AI and game development. I believe that these two fields have tremendous potential to shape the future and make a positive impact on society.
